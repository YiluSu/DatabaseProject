Welcome to the help menu for the upload page

This will allow you to add a radiologyrecord and upload the medicalimages into the radiology record.

You need to choose the doctor name and the patient name and add all the informations, then you are allowed to upload the images by the upload applet. You can hit the browse button to choose your images then click the upload button. After uploading the images you can click the upload button you can jump to the process confirmation page. All the radilogy record information display on the screen. You can click the continue button to confirm this recod then you can get the upload reslut informations

If you got some problem on it, try to set up your account.


Follow The instructions below:
  • Download the Applet jar file from here. Place it the top folder (proj1).
  • Download fileUpload jar, Commons-io jarfiles and place them under the WEB-INF/lib folder. If you don't have the lib folder, create it.
